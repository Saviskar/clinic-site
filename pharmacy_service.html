Pharmacy

Welcome to the Pharmacy at Meta Hospital, where we are committed to providing our patients with high-quality medications and exceptional service. Our pharmacy is stocked with a comprehensive range of prescription and over-the- counter medicines to meet the diverse healthcare needs of our community. Whether you require daily medication or specialized prescriptions, we ensure that you have convenient access to the treatment you need.

Our team of licensed pharmacists is always available to offer professional advice and answer any questions you may have about your medication. They work closely with doctors to ensure that prescriptions are accurate and tailored to your specific health requirements. We prioritize patient safety and take every measure to ensure that medications are dispensed responsibly and with the highest standards of care.



At Meta Hospital’s Pharmacy, we understand the importance of timely and efficient service. That’s why we strive to minimize wait times while maintaining the quality of our care. Our streamlined process ensures that you receive your medications promptly, so you can focus on your recovery and overall health. We also offer guidance on dosage, side effects, and the best ways to take your medication.

In addition to our in-house services, we provide a user-friendly platform for online orders, making it easy for patients to refill their prescriptions from the comfort of their homes. Whether you visit us in person or online, Meta Hospital’s Pharmacy is here to support your health journey with convenience, care, and expertise.
