Our Specialists

At Meta Hospital, we are proud to have a team of highly qualified and dedicated doctors who specialize in various fields of healthcare, ensuring our patients receive the best possible care. Dr. Nimal Perera, a leading pharmacist, oversees our Pharmacy Services, providing expert guidance on medications. In our Emergency Services, Dr. Sameera Wijesinghe is always ready to handle critical medical situations with precision and care. Our Surgery Services are led by Dr. Kavinda Senanayake, an accomplished surgeon known for his expertise in complex procedures. For outpatient care, Dr. Shalini Fernando offers attentive service through our Open Patient Services. Patients can consult Dr. Ruwanthi Silva via our Channelling Services to get specialized medical attention. Our Lab Services, guided by Dr. Deepthi Jayawardena, ensure accurate diagnostics through state-of-the-art testing. Dr. Kasun Wickramasinghe, an experienced physiotherapist, leads our Physiotherapy Services, helping patients with personalized rehabilitation plans. For heart care, Dr. Anura Jayasuriya, a top cardiologist, directs our Cardiology Services, providing comprehensive cardiovascular treatment. Finally, Dr. Menaka Rodrigo, a skilled obstetrician, offers compassionate care in our Maternity Services, supporting mothers through every step of their pregnancy journey. Together, these nine specialists bring unmatched expertise to Meta Hospital, ensuring the highest standard of care for every patient.
