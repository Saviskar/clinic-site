Our Branches


Meta Central

Located in the heart of the city, Meta Central offers comprehensive healthcare services with modern facilities. Equipped with advanced diagnostic tools, emergency care, and specialized departments, our central branch provides a full range of services to meet the diverse healthcare needs of the community. Our dedicated team ensures round-the-clock care for patients with complex medical conditions.



Meta North

Situated in the suburban area, Meta North provides accessible, quality healthcare closer to home. With a focus on outpatient services and preventive care, this branch is ideal for families seeking regular check-ups, vaccinations, and health screenings. Meta North also features a state-of-the-art maternity ward and pediatric care center, making it a trusted choice for growing families.



Meta South

Meta South is designed to deliver specialized care for chronic conditions and rehabilitation. With a focus on physical therapy, post-surgical care, and long-term treatments, this branch is equipped with the latest technology in rehabilitation and recovery. Our multidisciplinary team collaborates to provide personalized treatment plans to ensure optimal recovery and quality of life for our patients.
