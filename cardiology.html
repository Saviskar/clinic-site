Brief

At Meta Hospital, our cardiology department is committed to providing world-class care for all aspects of heart health. Our team of experienced cardiologists and cardiac specialists work together to offer a full range of services, from routine check-ups and preventive screenings to the diagnosis and treatment of complex heart conditions. With a focus on personalized care, we aim to protect your heart and improve your overall well-being.

We use the latest advancements in cardiology to ensure accurate diagnosis and effective treatments. Our comprehensive services include ECG, stress tests, echocardiograms, angiography, and interventional procedures such as stent placement and catheterization. Whether you’re dealing with high blood pressure, arrhythmia, or coronary artery disease, we provide a treatment plan that is tailored to your specific needs, with an emphasis on minimizing risks and promoting long-term heart health.



At Meta Hospital, we believe in the power of early detection and prevention. Our cardiology team offers heart health assessments and risk evaluations, helping you understand your cardiovascular health and take proactive steps to reduce potential risks. With lifestyle advice, medication management, and ongoing monitoring, we empower you to make informed decisions about your heart health.

Our goal is to provide compassionate, patient-centered care at every stage of your heart health journey. From diagnosis to recovery, our team is here to support you with expertise and guidance. With a focus on both acute and preventive care, Meta Hospital is your trusted partner in maintaining a healthy heart and improving your quality of life.
